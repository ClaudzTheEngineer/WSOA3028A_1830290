by Giovanni Suzzi

In these uncertain times, I wanna break this with something different. What can we offer one another to help each other? As a tech company, we have been working from home and are familiar with the tech out there, This time has proven to be a challenging time for everyone, especially those less fortunate. The biggest issue found to be plaguing me and my colleagues has been loss of motivation and work ethic. Whereby we’d spend hours staring at blank computer screens and getting no work done. This video was shared around the workplace. And encouraged good work life and home life balance. Perhaps it could help you, the reader, too. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=snAhsXyO3Ck.

In a continued effort to assist our local community, we at Suzzi Inc have decided to offer a discount on IT solutions and integration for small to medium sized businesses, to assist businesses that are struggling to make the jump to online only work.

Though we are doing fine, we are 6 paces away from struggle, but this doesn’t stop us from helping our community because we are all in the same boat. Suzzi Inc. has also bought a few thousand masks and distributed them amongst the local community. The folding@home initiative has been going great,

On a more personal note, me and my wife have been doing grocery runs for a few of the elderly people in our neighbourhood, especially those who cannot leave due to risk or otherwise. Every little bit helps.

To our readers, and to their loved ones - we wish you safety

The Suzzi Team
