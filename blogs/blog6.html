Suzzi Inc. during the Outbreak

by Giovanni Suzzi

During the current pandemic, as you may know, a nationwide lock-down has been enacted. As such all businesses that are not considered critical have been forced to close their doors. We here at Suzzi LTD have decided to self-isolate to assist in the prevention of spreading the virus. However, we will resume work in an online capacity to maintain deadlines with our clients. We will be unable to do workplace deployments until such time as the lockdown has been reduced after May 1st.

To assist in this transition, I have allowed various staff in the office to take their desktop workstations from the office to use at home if necessary. Luckily most of my staff have good internet connections, however, my one staff member does not have one, relying on a fixed data package. To help with this I went through the office networking supplies and retrieved a long-range wireless bridge we had used for an offsite project a few years ago. Using this I established a internet connection from their house to the office so that they may piggyback off the officeâ€™s business internet (Lucky her!).

To assist in combatting the virus globally, I have entered the workplace into the Folding@Home initiative. This way we can help contribute to the research for COVID-19. To do this I pulled out all the workstation PCs out of storage and the remaining workstations to be permanently be computing for Folding@Home. This means about 256 threads of raw performance.

To everyone else at home, from us at Suzzi we wish you safety for you and your loved ones
